Technology and Development Skills

  • Fast Feedback

    Tools

    Fast Feedback is a desktop application built with React with CSS Modules on the front end, Node/Express on the back end, as well as MongoDB.

    Use

    Sign in using Google Auth. Add credits using Stripe API. Create surveys from within the application using Redux Form and the app will send an email to your clients. Recipients can cast a vote from within their email. The app will tabulate votes for you.

  • My Day App

    Tools

    Use

Track current weather for your location, check out current events in your area, search the internet Get inspired through a random quote of the day, as well as a new background every time you refresh the page Organize your tasks through to do functionality of the app that persists due to Firebase Database.
